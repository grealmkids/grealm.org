WHO WE ARE

G-Realm studio was founded by Alfred Ochola and his beautiful wife Mrs. Claire Ochola.

Together, they have a burning desire to equip all the young children of Africa with knowledge and practical life skills, and to raise them to become God-loving and God-fearing people. This way, they will make Africa and the world at large a beatiful place for everybody to live in. We believe in and stand for the LORD and Saviour of all mankind: Jesus Christ, the son of God.

Claire has a bachelors degree in music while Alfred is a software and computer art specialist. Our team has other key contributors like D.J Tonny, Don Stuart, Joel, Prosper International, the G-Realm kids (Agaba, Precious, Patience, Paroma, Timothy, Annet) and many more wonderful people.
